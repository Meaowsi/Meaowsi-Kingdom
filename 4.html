Understanding Microcontrollers

Published on: August 10, 2024

Microcontrollers are tiny computers embedded in devices to perform specific tasks. They consist of a processor, memory, and input/output peripherals. In this post, we delve into how microcontrollers work, their applications, and why they are integral to modern electronics.

Microcontrollers are found in everything from household appliances to automotive systems. Their ability to be programmed for specific functions makes them highly versatile. We will explore various types of microcontrollers, common use cases, and best practices for programming them.
